10. Summary Judgment
The system is no longer “mid-build” — it is near production-ready for a controlled pilot.
Biggest remaining value is configuration (Gemini, SMTP, SMS, migrations) not greenfield coding.
Biggest remaining quality risks: fee math approximations, SMS placeholder, RLS looseness, incomplete notification wiring.
AI MCQ quizzes are a differentiator and already implemented with free Gemini — activate with your key.
Recommended immediate path: A1–A5 this week, then B1/B3/B8, then expand notifications and parent scores.
